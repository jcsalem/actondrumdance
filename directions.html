Directions to NARA Park

NARA Park is located in Acton, Massachusetts.
Park in the lot off Quarry Road (not LedgeRock Way)
Our fire circle will be in the corner of the soccer field across from the parking lot.

From 495N

  • Take Exit 29A (to Rte 2 East).
  • Take Exit 42 (Rte 27 Acton, Maynard).
  • Take left at end of the ramp. Follow Rte 27.
  • Go straight through 2 sets of lights. Approximately one mile from the second set of lights, turn left on Quarry Road.
  • Follow Quary Road about 1/2 mile to the NARA Park parking lot on your right.

From Rte 128

  • Take Exit 29 (Rte 2 West).
  • Take Exit 41 (Rte 27 Acton, Maynard).
  • Take right at the end of ramp. Follow Rte 27.
  • Go straight through 2 sets of lights. Approximately one mile from the second set of lights, turn left on Quarry Road.
  • Follow Quary Road about 1/2 mile to the NARA Park parking lot on your right.
